# ⌨️ Windows Virtual-Key (VK) Codes Reference

A quick reference for commonly used Windows Virtual-Key (VK) codes.

---

## 🔤 Letters (A–Z)

Letters are sequential, starting at `0x41`.

| Key | Hex | Key | Hex | Key | Hex |
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| A | `0x41` | J | `0x4A` | S | `0x53` |
| B | `0x42` | K | `0x4B` | T | `0x54` |
| C | `0x43` | L | `0x4C` | U | `0x55` |
| D | `0x44` | M | `0x4D` | V | `0x56` |
| E | `0x45` | N | `0x4E` | W | `0x57` |
| F | `0x46` | O | `0x4F` | X | `0x58` |
| G | `0x47` | P | `0x50` | Y | `0x59` |
| H | `0x48` | Q | `0x51` | Z | `0x5A` |
| I | `0x49` | R | `0x52` |  |  |

---

## 🔢 Number Keys (Top Row)

Numbers are sequential, starting at `0x30`.

| Key | Hex | Key | Hex | Key | Hex | Key | Hex | Key | Hex |
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| 0 | `0x30` | 2 | `0x32` | 4 | `0x34` | 6 | `0x36` | 8 | `0x38` |
| 1 | `0x31` | 3 | `0x33` | 5 | `0x35` | 7 | `0x37` | 9 | `0x39` |

---

## 🔢 Numpad Keys

| Key | Hex |
|------|------|
| Numpad 0–9 | `0x60` → `0x69` |
| Numpad `*` | `0x6A` |
| Numpad `+` | `0x6B` |
| Numpad `-` | `0x6D` |
| Numpad `.` | `0x6E` |
| Numpad `/` | `0x6F` |

---

## 🎛️ Function Keys

Function keys are sequential, starting at `0x70`.

| Key | Hex | Key | Hex |
|------|------|------|------|
| F1 | `0x70` | F7 | `0x76` |
| F2 | `0x71` | F8 | `0x77` |
| F3 | `0x72` | F9 | `0x78` |
| F4 | `0x73` | F10 | `0x79` |
| F5 | `0x74` | F11 | `0x7A` |
| F6 | `0x75` | F12 | `0x7B` |

> **Note:** `F13`–`F24` continue sequentially through `0x87`.

---

> **Tip:** Sequential ranges make it easy to remember:
>
> - **A–Z:** `0x41` → `0x5A`
> - **0–9:** `0x30` → `0x39`
> - **Numpad:** `0x60` → `0x69`
> - **F1–F24:** `0x70` → `0x87`
